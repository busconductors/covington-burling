Waiver & NDA Forms

Downloadable legal documents prepared by Covington & Burling LLP. Available as PDF documents and editable LaTeX source files.

These templates are provided for general informational purposes. For matters requiring legal advice specific to your situation, please contact our office to schedule a consultation.

Waiver and Release of Liability

A release of liability form for client matters. Fill in the client name, date, and matter description, then sign. Suitable for general engagement purposes.

Includes: Acknowledgment of Risk, Release, Indemnification, Governing Law (DC), Entire Agreement

Download LaTeX Source

Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

A mutual NDA for protecting confidential information between parties. Fill in client name, address, and effective date, then both parties sign.

Includes: Definition of Confidential Info, Obligations, Exclusions, Term (2 years), Governing Law (DC)

Download LaTeX Source

PDF generation requires the server to be running. If the server is unavailable, the LaTeX source files can be compiled locally with pdflatex.

How to Use These Forms

  1. Download the form. Choose PDF for a ready-to-print document or LaTeX (.tex) for an editable source file.
  2. Fill in the fields. Enter the client name, date, matter description (waiver), or client address (NDA) in the designated fields.
  3. Review and sign. Both parties should review the document carefully before signing. For the NDA, both Covington & Burling LLP and the client must sign.
  4. Compile LaTeX files. To edit and recompile the .tex files, use pdflatex waiver.tex or pdflatex nda.tex with a standard LaTeX distribution.
  5. Consult an attorney. These templates are provided as starting points. Consult with qualified legal counsel before signing any legal document.
