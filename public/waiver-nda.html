How to Use These Forms

  1. Download the fillable PDF. Click the download button above for the form you need. The PDF will open in any standard PDF reader (Adobe Acrobat, Preview, or your browser).
  2. Fill in the form fields. Click on each field to enter the client name, date, matter description, and other required information. The PDF form fields are interactive and can be filled in on any device.
  3. Save your completed form. After filling in all fields, save the PDF. The filled-in data will be preserved in the document.
  4. Review and sign. Both parties should review the document carefully before signing. For the NDA, both Covington & Burling LLP and the client must sign.
  5. Consult an attorney. These templates are provided as starting points. Consult with qualified legal counsel before signing any legal document.
