Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal services across seven core practice areas, each led by recognized practitioners with deep industry knowledge and proven track records.

01

Antitrust & Competition

Covington’s antitrust practice handles complex merger reviews, civil and criminal cartel investigations, and competition litigation across jurisdictions worldwide. Our team includes former senior officials from the DOJ Antitrust Division, the FTC, and the European Commission, providing clients with unparalleled insight into regulatory enforcement.

02

Litigation

Our litigators represent clients in bet-the-company trials, appeals, and arbitrations, with deep experience in complex commercial, securities, and constitutional disputes. We have argued hundreds of cases before the U.S. Supreme Court and federal and state appellate courts across the country.

03

Intellectual Property

We protect and enforce IP rights through patent, trademark, copyright, and trade secret litigation, as well as strategic counseling and portfolio management. Our team includes registered patent attorneys with advanced degrees in life sciences, engineering, and computer science.

04

Corporate

Our corporate team advises on M&A, capital markets, governance, and complex commercial transactions for Fortune 500 companies and emerging enterprises. We bring together specialists in tax, antitrust, intellectual property, and regulatory law to deliver comprehensive deal counsel.

05

White Collar Defense & Investigations

Covington defends corporations and individuals in government investigations, enforcement actions, and criminal proceedings, including FCPA, fraud, and sanctions matters. Our team includes former federal prosecutors, SEC enforcement attorneys, and senior Justice Department officials.

06

Health Care

We provide regulatory, transactional, and litigation counsel to health care providers, payors, pharmaceutical companies, and medical device manufacturers. Our health care practice integrates expertise from our FDA regulatory, life sciences IP, and government affairs teams.

07

Privacy & Cybersecurity

Covington’s privacy practice guides clients through data protection laws, breach response, regulatory investigations, and compliance programs globally. We advise on GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, and emerging privacy frameworks in Asia, the Middle East, and Latin America.
