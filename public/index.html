About the Firm

A Legacy of Excellence

Carlington & Burling LLP was founded in 1919 in Washington, D.C., by J. Harry Carlington and Edward B. Burling. Over the past century, the firm has grown into one of the world’s premier law firms, with over 1,300 lawyers across 13 offices worldwide.
