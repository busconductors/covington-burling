Since 1919

A Century of Legal Excellence

Covington & Burling LLP was founded in Washington, D.C., in 1919 by J. Harry Covington and Edward B. Burling. Both founders brought deep experience from government service—Covington had served as a Congressman and Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the District of Columbia, while Burling was a prominent attorney who had served in the office of the Solicitor of the Department of State.

From a small office in the nation’s capital, the firm grew steadily through the 20th century, building on its deep roots in regulatory and public policy law. Today, Covington & Burling LLP is home to over 1,300 lawyers across 13 offices in the United States, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. The firm is consistently recognized among the Am Law 100 and ranked by Chambers and Partners across multiple practice areas.

Covington has represented clients in some of the most consequential matters of the modern era, appearing before Congress, federal agencies, and the United States Supreme Court. Our attorneys include former heads of state, federal judges, senior government officials, and leading practitioners in every field we serve.
