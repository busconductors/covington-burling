% ---- INTRO ----
\noindent THIS WAIVER AND RELEASE OF LIABILITY (the ``Release'') is made as of
\EffectiveDate\ by and between \textbf{Covington \& Burling LLP} and
\textbf{\ClientName} (the ``Client''). The Client acknowledges having read
and understood each provision of this Release.

\vspace{0.4cm}
% ---- RECITALS ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{RECITALS}}

\vspace{0.2cm}
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy!70!black}{\textbf{WHEREAS}}, the Client has
engaged Covington \& Burling LLP in connection with the matter identified as
\textbf{\MatterName} (the ``Matter'');

\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy!70!black}{\textbf{WHEREAS}}, the Client
acknowledges and accepts all risks inherent in legal proceedings and the
provision of legal services, and wishes to release the Firm from liability as
set forth herein;

\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy!70!black}{\textbf{NOW, THEREFORE}}, in
consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as
follows:

\vspace{0.4cm}
% ---- SECTION 1 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{1. Acknowledgment of Risk}}

\noindent The Client acknowledges that all legal matters involve inherent risks
and uncertainties. The Client understands that Covington \& Burling LLP makes
no guarantees regarding specific outcomes, and that past results do not
guarantee future results. The Client has been advised of the potential risks
associated with the Matter and voluntarily assumes all such risks.

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 2 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{2. Release}}

\noindent To the fullest extent permitted by law, the Client hereby releases,
waives, and discharges Covington \& Burling LLP, its partners, associates,
employees, and agents from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, and
causes of action arising out of or related to the Matter, except for claims
arising from gross negligence or willful misconduct on the part of the Firm.

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 3 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{3. Indemnification}}

\noindent The Client agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless
Covington \& Burling LLP, its partners, associates, employees, and agents from
and against any and all third-party claims, liabilities, damages, losses, and
expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or in connection
with the Client's actions or omissions in connection with the Matter.

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 4 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{4. Governing Law}}

\noindent This Release shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of the District of Columbia, without regard to its conflict of laws
principles. Any dispute arising under this Release shall be resolved exclusively
in the courts of the District of Columbia.

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 5 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{5. Entire Agreement and Miscellaneous}}

\noindent \textbf{5.1 Entire Agreement.} This Release constitutes the entire
agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and
supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether oral or written.

\noindent \textbf{5.2 Amendments.} No amendment to this Release shall be
effective unless in writing and signed by both Parties.

\noindent \textbf{5.3 Severability.} If any provision of this Release is held
invalid, the remainder shall continue in full force and effect.

\noindent \textbf{5.4 No Waiver.} The failure of either Party to enforce any
provision shall not constitute a waiver of such provision or any other
provision.
