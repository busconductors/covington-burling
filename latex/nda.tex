% ---- INTRO ----
\noindent THIS NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (the ``Agreement'') is made as of
\EffectiveDate\ (the ``Effective Date''), by and between
\textbf{Covington \& Burling LLP}, with offices at 850 Tenth Street NW,
Washington, DC 20001 (the ``Firm''), and \textbf{\ClientName}, with an address
at \textbf{\ClientAddress} (the ``Client''). Each may be referred to as a
``Party'' and collectively as the ``Parties.''

\vspace{0.4cm}
% ---- RECITALS ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{RECITALS}}

\vspace{0.2cm}
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy!70!black}{\textbf{WHEREAS}}, the Parties wish to
explore a potential business relationship (the ``Purpose'') and may disclose
certain proprietary information to each other in furtherance thereof;

\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy!70!black}{\textbf{WHEREAS}}, the Parties wish to
ensure that any such information remains confidential and is used solely for
the Purpose;

\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy!70!black}{\textbf{NOW, THEREFORE}}, in
consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as
follows:

\vspace{0.4cm}
% ---- SECTION 1 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{1. Definition of Confidential Information}}

\noindent \textbf{1.1 Confidential Information.} For purposes of this
Agreement, ``Confidential Information'' means any information, whether oral,
written, or electronic, disclosed by one Party (the ``Disclosing Party'') to
the other (the ``Receiving Party'') that is designated as confidential or that
reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the
information and the circumstances of disclosure. Confidential Information
includes but is not limited to: business plans, financial data, customer lists,
technical data, trade secrets, product roadmaps, and proprietary methodologies.

\noindent \textbf{1.2 Exclusions.} Confidential Information does not include
information that:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item is or becomes publicly known through no fault of the Receiving Party;
  \item was rightfully in the Receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure;
  \item is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of the
        Disclosing Party's Confidential Information;
  \item is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the
        Receiving Party gives prompt notice to the Disclosing Party.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 2 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{2. Obligations of the Receiving Party}}

\noindent The Receiving Party shall:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;
  \item not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the
        Disclosing Party's prior written consent;
  \item use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
  \item exercise at least a reasonable degree of care in protecting the
        Confidential Information.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 3 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{3. Term and Termination}}

\noindent \textbf{3.1 Term.} This Agreement shall commence on the Effective
Date and continue for a period of two (2) years, unless terminated earlier by
mutual written agreement.

\noindent \textbf{3.2 Survival.} The obligations of confidentiality and non-use
shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 4 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{4. Governing Law}}

\noindent This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of the District of Columbia. Any disputes arising hereunder shall be
resolved exclusively in the federal or state courts located in the District of
Columbia, and each Party consents to the personal jurisdiction and venue of such
courts.

\vspace{0.3cm}
% ---- SECTION 5 ----
\noindent \textcolor{DarkNavy}{\large \textbf{5. Miscellaneous}}

\noindent \textbf{5.1 Entire Agreement.} This Agreement constitutes the entire
understanding between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and
supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether oral or written.

\noindent \textbf{5.2 Amendments.} No amendment shall be binding unless in
writing and signed by both Parties.

\noindent \textbf{5.3 Severability.} If any provision is held invalid, the
remainder shall continue in full force and effect.

\noindent \textbf{5.4 No Waiver.} The failure of either Party to enforce any
provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of such provision or
any other provision.
